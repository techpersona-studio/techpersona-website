## Technical SEO

### Robots.txt — Pass

Present at `/robots.txt` (200 OK). `User-agent: * / Allow: /`. Sitemap declared correctly. No disallow rules, no crawl-delay issues. No action needed.

### Sitemap — Low severity

`/sitemap.xml` returns 200 and contains 3 valid URLs. `sitemap_index.xml` returns 404, which is not a problem for a 3-URL site. Two gaps worth addressing:

- No image sitemap. The before/after screenshot (`old-quanphoinsaugus.com_.png`) and profile photos are not discoverable for Google Image Search without `<image:image>` entries.
- No video sitemap. Three `.mp4` demo files are embedded directly. Without `<video:video>` entries, Google Video won't index them.

**Current status:** Acceptable for now. The main sitemap is live and valid. Image/video sitemap extensions are optional next-phase work, not blockers.

### Canonical tags — Pass

Homepage carries `<link rel="canonical" href="https://www.techpersonastudio.com/">` — self-referencing, correct, WWW canonical confirmed. Confirm the privacy policy and terms pages carry self-referencing canonicals.

### HTTPS — Pass

Fully HTTPS. HTTP/2 confirmed. HSTS at `max-age=63072000` (2 years). No mixed content visible in HTML. No action needed.

### Crawlability — Medium severity

Two issues:

- **Vietnamese toggle is JavaScript-only.** The VI/EN toggle is driven by client-side JS with `data-i18n` attributes. No separate URL is served for Vietnamese content. Googlebot may not reliably index the Vietnamese state.

**Current status:** Favicon is fixed. The only remaining crawlability decision is whether Vietnamese needs real indexable URLs or is fine as a user-facing toggle only.

### Indexability — Pass

No `noindex` tags on the homepage or service pages. The privacy policy and terms pages are intentionally `noindex, follow`, which is correct for legal pages. Robots.txt blocks nothing.

### Page speed signals — Medium severity
