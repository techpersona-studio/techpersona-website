The vibe

You're hiring a person, not an agency.

Pine Editorial should feel like a calm, capable studio run by one named human. Warm editorial moments with an operational backbone, serious enough for a law firm, grounded enough for a roofer, and never mistakable for a SaaS startup.

Founder-led, not faceless

A real person carries the brand. First-person voice, a real face, plain owner-language.

Calm, not loud

One rare accent holds all the warmth. Pine and stone do the rest of the work.

Premium, not precious

Soft depth, generous space, restrained color. Expensive, never over-art-directed.

Operational, not decorative

Outcomes an owner gets in five seconds. The technology stays behind the wall.

02
Typography

Hanken leads. Fraunces accents.

Hanken Grotesk does ~90% of the work and keeps the brand operationally competent. Fraunces is the premium accent for hero moments, and the italic phrase is the signature, used once per section, not for whole sentences.

Fraunces · accent · ~10% usage
Built for owners who value response time.

Use Fraunces for hero headlines and a single emphasized phrase. The italic, set in terracotta, is the brand's signature move.

Rules

Sentence case for headings. Tight tracking on display (-.045em). Uppercase only for small tracked kickers. No serif body, no Title Case, no magazine layouts.

Components

Double-bezel cards, island buttons, soft depth.

04
What to avoid

The fastest ways to weaken the brand.

  • ✕ Hard offset block shadows. The biggest "indie agency" tell, use soft, pine-tinted depth only.
  • ✕ Purple/blue AI gradients, glassmorphism, or frosted blur on content.
  • ✕ Workflow/node diagrams, dashboards, integration logos. The tech stays behind the wall.
  • ✕ Pure white fields, a second accent color, or large fields of terracotta.
  • ✕ Serif body or serif everywhere. Fraunces is a 10% accent.
  • ✕ Fake metrics, fake case studies, faceless-agency framing.

● Do

  • Lead with the founder and the outcome
  • Use pine as the main trust signal
  • Keep terracotta rare and warm
  • Soft depth, squircle corners
  • Hanken for reading, Fraunces for moments
  • Speak plainly to owners

✕ Don't

  • Look like a generic AI agency
  • Look like a brutalist design studio
  • Overuse Fraunces or terracotta
  • Show the machinery
  • Use corporate jargon
  • Optimize for only one vertical
